How It Works

From installation to your digital twin in 5 simple steps.

1

Create Your Account

Install OpenEgo locally and set up your profile. No email required, no cloud signup.

2

Select Permissions

Choose which platforms to connect - Gmail, Telegram, Slack, and local files. You're in complete control.

3

Pick Your AI Model

Select from local models like Llama 3, Mistral, or Phi-3. All run on your device, no API calls.

4

Learn & Build

OpenEgo scans your historical data and builds your communication model. Takes 1-2 days depending on data size.

5

Test & Activate

Pass validation tests to prove accuracy. Then choose Co-Pilot or Auto-Pilot mode. You're ready!
